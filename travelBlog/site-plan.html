Travel Blog Site Plan

Emily Hatch

WDD 131-02

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my site will be to provide images and personal experience with travel. It is more to put all my information and photos in one spot than to guide.

Audience

It will be less educational, and more personal with a audience of people who are curious about my travels.

Dynamic elements

I will use javascript to create a slideshow of images as well as being able to filter blog posts by location.
